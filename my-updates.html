Completely move-in ready. This is in a quiet cul-de-sac and yet very close to shopping, schools, and easy access to major roads. New wood kitchen cabinets and granite countertops brand new stainless steel appliances. Big corner lot with amazing landscaping. Open floor plan, allowing plenty of natural light, with skylights through the living room and primary bathroom. New double panel windows to keep cool in and the heat out.

A covered front porch and a wood hand-made Gazebo in the rear patio. Bathrooms were completely remodeled with new wood vanities, granite countertops, and new tile. The community has 2 parks with playgrounds. The community park, set on 10 acres, is open from sunrise to sunset and provides a grilling area, playground, and restroom facilities. And the most important NO HOA!!!
